      The Artist

                  

Theo Caloyer is a 19 year old American artist from Pittsburgh Pennsylvania. He grew up in the Shadyside area, and attended Kentucky Avenue School where he developed his early interests in painting. He then attended Central Catholic High School, where he developed his artistic style further, branching out into pop, and street art. Theo now attends Chatham University, and is majoring in Fine Arts. His current influences stem from a variety of different artists, ranging from Picasso to Alec Monopoly.
